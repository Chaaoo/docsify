lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日起<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>,</SPAN>全国范围内所有缴纳营业税的企业全面实施营业税改征增值税（以下称营改增）。自此，营业税正式退出历史舞台，增值税覆盖全国所有行业。本次营改增是<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1994</SPAN>年迄今影响最大的一次税制改革。由于增值税与营业税在税收原理、计量方法、纳税义务认定、会计核算等诸多方面存在较大差异，因此，税制转换会对<SPAN

lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal

lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal

face=Calibri>针对税制转换对<SPAN

lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><B

style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>一、营改增前后跨期发票抵扣风险<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>（一）营改增企业<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>对于本轮营改增纳税人而言，有可能存在两方面问题：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>1.</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>进项税额抵扣不合规。有的营改增企业于<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>30</SPAN>日前采购商品并投入使用，但要求销售方提供<SPAN lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日后的增值税专用发票，并据此认证抵扣销项税额。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>该行为应按照《中华人民共和国增值税暂行条例》第十条第一款的规定，不得抵扣进项或将此进项予以转出。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>2.</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>增值税专用发票开具不合规。营改增企业于<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>30</SPAN>日前提供了服务，应缴纳营业税并开具营业税发票。为使购买方（原增值税企业）增加可抵扣的进项税额，营改增企业于<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日后才开具专用发票。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>该行为属于未按发票管理办法开具发票，存在极大的税务行政处罚风险。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>（二）增值税企业<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>对于原增值税企业而言，与上述情形相对应，也存在两类问题：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>1.</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>企业纳税义务于<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>30</SPAN>日前已经产生，为让对方在营改增后增加进项，故延至<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日后才开具增值税专用发票。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>该行为属于未按发票管理办法开具发票，存在极大的税收行政处罚风险。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>2.</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>已接受营改增企业于<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>30</SPAN>日前提供的服务，为了增加进项，要求营改增企业延至<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日后开具增值税专用发票，并据此抵扣。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>该行为应根据《关于全面推开营业税改征增值税试点有关税收征收管理事项的公告》第三点第七款规定，对于营改增企业在营改增前已产生了纳税义务的，应在地税机关申报缴纳营业税。如未开具发票，<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日以后需要补开发票的，可于<SPAN lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>12</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>31</SPAN>日前开具增值税普通发票，而非增值税专用发票。所以，对于取得的专用发票，同样应在专用发票认证后作进项税额转出。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>注册会计师在审计中，需对<SPAN lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日前后的交易事项实施截止测试。应结合相应采购合同以及入库验收单的审查，确定被审计单位<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日之后认证的专用发票是否符合增值税管理规定；对被审计单位<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日之前发生的购进业务，在<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日之后取得增值税专用发票进行抵扣的，应按规定作进项税额转出处理。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><B 
style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>二、取得不合规增值税发票的风险<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>国家税务总局《关于全面推开营业税改征增值税试点有关税收征收管理事项的公告》，对发票开具的某些细节进行了详细规定，纳税人应严格遵守。公告第四条第三款规定，纳税人取得建筑业的增值税发票，备注栏应注明建筑服务发生地县（市、区）名称及项目名称；第四款规定，纳税人取得不动产销售发票，“货物或应税劳务、服务名称”栏应填写不动产名称及房屋产权证书号码（无房屋产权证书的可不填写），“单位”栏填写面积单位，备注栏应注明不动产的详细地址。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>对于纳税人以不符合要求的增值税专用发票进行抵扣的，税务机关在检查时会要求企业作进项税额转出并予以处罚。因此，注册会计师在<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年度财务报表审计中，应关注企业所取得发票的开具细节。如果发现存在上述问题，应提请被审计企业更换发票或进项税额转出，并在计算企业所得税时作相应纳税调整。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><B 
style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>三、取得不合规营业税发票的风险<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>在税制转换期，基于方便纳税人的特殊考虑，对于原营业税发票的使用时效，国家税务总局《关于全面推开营业税改征增值税试点有关税收征收管理事项的公告》规定：“原营业税发票原则上使用至<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>6</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>30</SPAN>日，特殊情况经省国税局确定，可适当延长使用期限，最迟不超过<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>8</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>31</SPAN>日。”<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>基于上述规定，注册会计师在审计过程中应关注企业取得的营业税发票日期。如为不合规票据，则存在不得在企业所得税税前扣除的风险。注册会计师应及时提请企业更换发票，或在计算企业所得税时作相应调整。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><B 
style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>四、增值税会计处理的变化及涉税风险<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>2016</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>年<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>12</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>3</SPAN>日，财政部出台《关于印发<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>&lt;</SPAN>增值税会计处理规定<SPAN lang=EN-US>&gt;</SPAN>的通知》（财会（<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>）<SPAN lang=EN-US>22</SPAN>号）（以下称<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>22</SPAN>号文），对增值税会计处理进行了规定。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>（一）本轮营改增企业<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>营改增前，建筑、金融等行业普遍存在较大金额的“应交税费<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>-</SPAN>应交营业税”的贷方余额（以下简称“营业税贷方余额”）。此部分贷方余额，一部分是应交未交营业税，另一部分是税法和会计核算差异形成的时间性差异。在<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>22</SPAN>号文出台前，对于该部分贷方余额，企业的处理方式均不相同。有的企业挂账，有的企业将应交营业税调整为应交增值税，有的企业参照会计政策变更，对以前年度营业收入及工程施工与工程结算进行追溯调整。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>注册会计师在实施<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年度财务报表审计时，对于营业税贷方余额应全面了解分析，根据是否形成了营业税纳税义务进行区分，并按照<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>22</SPAN>号文的要求处理。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>如果对<SPAN lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>30</SPAN>日之前已产生营业税纳税义务的营业税贷方余额，属于应交未交营业税，应提请被审计单位及时补缴，在税制转换时无需进行账务调整。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>对税法和会计核算差异形成的贷方余额，根据<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>22</SPAN>号文规定，应按如下方式处理：企业营改增前已确认收入，但因未产生营业税纳税义务而未计提营业税的，在达到增值税纳税义务时点时，企业应在确认应交增值税销项税额的同时冲减当期收入；已经计提营业税且未缴纳的，在达到增值税纳税义务时点时，应借记“应交税费—应交营业税”、“应交税费—应交城市维护建设税”、“应交税费—应交教育费附加”等科目，贷记“主营业务收入”科目，并根据调整后的收入计算确定计入“应交税费—待转销项税额”科目的金额<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>,</SPAN>同时冲减收入。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>（二）原增值税企业<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>22</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>号文规定：自<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日开始，原增值税企业的“营业税金及附加”科目名称调整为“税金及附加”。该科目核算企业经营活动发生的消费税、城市维护建设税、资源税、教育费附加及房产税、土地使用税、车船税、印花税等相关税费；利润表中的“营业税金及附加”项目调整为“税金及附加”。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>根据上述规定，企业应将<SPAN lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日至<SPAN lang=EN-US>12</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>31</SPAN>日间发生的，原在“管理费用”科目中核算的房产税、土地使用税、车船税、印花税调整至“税金及附加”科目核算。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><B 
style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>五、纳税主体及计税方法的变化及涉税风险<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>2016</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>年度出台了大量的营改增相关法规，建议对以下在纳税主体及计税方法方面发生的变化予以重点关注：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>(</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>一<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>) 
</SPAN>营改增后，根据《关于全面推开营业税改征增值税试点的通知》附件一第十四条规定，企业间无偿资金借贷，需视同销售缴纳增值税。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>(</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>二<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>) 2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>8</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>29</SPAN>号，国家税务总局《关于营改增试点若干征管问题的公告》第五条明确提出，企业转让限售股的所得，应缴纳增值税，并按限售股的不同形成原因分别计算其转让成本（买入价）。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><FONT 
face=Calibri><SPAN lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>(</SPAN><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'>三<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>) 2016</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>12</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>21</SPAN>日，国家税务总局《关于明确金融、房地产开发、教育辅助服务等增值税政策的通知》第四点规定，资管产品运营过程中发生的增值税应税行为，以资管产品管理人为增值税纳税人。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2017</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>6</SPAN>日发布《关于资管产品增值税政策有关问题的补充通知》规定，上述纳税主体的<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>2017</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>7</SPAN>月<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>日（含）以后执行。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><B 
style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>六、税制转换期的重复纳税风险<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN 
style='FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; mso-hansi-font-family: "Arial Narrow"; mso-bidi-font-size: 16.0pt'><FONT 
face=Calibri>税制转换期间，下面一些特殊经济业务，容易被忽视而导致重复交税，注册会计师审计中应予以专注。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN

face=Calibri>（一）四类预收业务<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal 
style="MARGIN: 7.8pt 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 125%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; mso-para-margin-top: .5gd; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0"><SPAN

lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月<SPAN

lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月<SPAN

<P class=MsoNormal

<P class=MsoNormal
