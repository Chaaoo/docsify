<P

<P class=title

<P

style="FONT-SIZE: 13pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #333333"><FONT 
face=微软雅黑>第五条</FONT></SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><A style="BOX-SIZING: border-box"

style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">鉴证业务是指注册会计师对鉴证对象信息提出结论，以增强除责任方之外的预期使用者对鉴证对象信息信任程度的业务。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN

<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

<P

<P

</FONT></SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><A style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" 
name=No21_Z2T7K1></A><FONT size=3><SPAN class=sect2content><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><SPAN 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">鉴证业务分为基于责任方认定的业务和直接报告业务。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>在基于责任方认定的业务中，责任方对鉴证对象进行评价或计量，鉴证对象信息以责任方认定的形式为预期使用者获取。如在财务报表审计中，被审计单位管理层（责任方）对财务状况、经营成果和现金流量（鉴证对象）进行确认、计量和列报（评价或计量）而形成的财务报表（鉴证对象信息）即为责任方的认定，该财务报表可为预期报表使用者获取，注册会计师针对财务报表出具审计报告。这种业务属于基于责任方认定的业务。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No23_Z2T7K3></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>在直接报告业务中，注册会计师直接对鉴证对象进行评价或计量，或者从责任方获取对鉴证对象评价或计量的认定，而该认定无法为预期使用者获取，预期使用者只能通过阅读鉴证报告获取鉴证对象信息。如在内部控制鉴证业务中，注册会计师可能无法从管理层（责任方）获取其对内部控制有效性的评价报告（责任方认定），或虽然注册会计师能够获取该报告，但预期使用者无法获取该报告，注册会计师直接对内部控制的有效性（鉴证对象）进行评价并出具鉴证报告，预期使用者只能通过阅读该鉴证报告获得内部控制有效性的信息（鉴证对象信息）。这种业务属于直接报告业务。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN

style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">鉴证业务的保证程度分为合理保证和有限保证。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>合理保证的鉴证业务的目标是注册会计师将鉴证业务风险降至该业务环境下可接受的低水平，以此作为以积极方式提出结论的基础。如在历史财务信息审计中，要求注册会计师将审计风险降至可接受的低水平，对审计后的历史财务信息提供高水平保证（合理保证），在审计报告中对历史财务信息采用积极方式提出结论。这种业务属于合理保证的鉴证业务。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>有限保证的鉴证业务的目标是注册会计师将鉴证业务风险降至该业务环境下可接受的水平，以此作为以消极方式提出结论的基础。如在历史财务信息审阅中，要求注册会计师将审阅风险降至该业务环境下可接受的水平（高于历史财务信息审计中可接受的低水平），对审阅后的历史财务信息提供低于高水平的保证（有限保证），在审阅报告中对历史财务信息采用消极方式提出结论。这种业务属于有限保证的鉴证业务。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>

lang=EN-US

<P class=sect2content1

<P class=title

<P class=title

<P class=title

<P class=title

<P class=title

<P class=sect2content1

style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box"><A style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" 
name=No127_Z7J3T32></A><SPAN class=sect2title><SPAN 
style="FONT-SIZE: 13pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #333333"><FONT 
face=微软雅黑>第三十二条</FONT></SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><A style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" 
name=No128_Z7J3T32K1></A><FONT size=3><SPAN class=sect2content><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><SPAN 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">证据的充分性是对证据数量的衡量，主要与注册会计师确定的样本量有关。证据的适当性是对证据质量的衡量，即证据的相关性和可靠性。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No129_Z7J3T32K2></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>所需证据的数量受鉴证对象信息重大错报风险的影响，即风险越大，可能需要的证据数量越多；所需证据的数量也受证据质量的影响，即证据质量越高，可能需要的证据数量越少。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No130_Z7J3T32K3></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>尽管证据的充分性和适当性相关，但如果证据的质量存在缺陷，注册会计师仅靠获取更多的证据可能无法弥补其质量上的缺陷。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box"><A style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" 
name=No131_Z7J3T33></A><SPAN class=sect2title><SPAN 
style="FONT-SIZE: 13pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal; COLOR: #333333"><FONT 
face=微软雅黑>第三十三条</FONT></SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><A style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" 
name=No132_Z7J3T33K1></A><FONT size=3><SPAN class=sect2content><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><SPAN 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">证据的可靠性受其来源和性质的影响，并取决于获取证据的具体环境。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No133_Z7J3T33K2></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>注册会计师通常按照下列原则考虑证据的可靠性：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No134_Z7J3T33K2X1></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>（一）从外部独立来源获取的证据比从其他来源获取的证据更可靠；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No135_Z7J3T33K2X2></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>（二）内部控制有效时内部生成的证据比内部控制薄弱时内部生成的证据更可靠；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No136_Z7J3T33K2X3></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>（三）直接获取的证据比间接获取或推论得出的证据更可靠；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No137_Z7J3T33K2X4></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>（四）以文件记录形式（无论是纸质、电子或其他介质）存在的证据比口头形式的证据更可靠；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>（五）从原件获取的证据比从传真或复印件获取的证据更可靠。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No139_Z7J3T33K3></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>在运用本条第二款第（一）项至第（五）项所述原则评价证据的可靠性时，注册会计师应当注意可能出现的重大例外情况。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN

style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">如果针对某项认定从不同来源获取的证据或获取的不同性质的证据能够相互印证，与该项认定相关的证据通常具有更强的说服力。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

size=3>如果从不同来源获取的证据或获取的不同性质的证据不一致，可能表明某项证据不可靠，注册会计师应当追加必要的程序予以解决。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN

style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">针对一个期间的鉴证对象信息获取充分、适当的证据，通常要比针对一个时点的鉴证对象信息获取充分、适当的证据更困难。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN

style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">注册会计师可以考虑获取证据的成本与所获取信息有用性之间的关系，但不应仅以获取证据的困难和成本为由减少不可替代的程序。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US

<P class=sect2content1

lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN

<P

<P class=sect2content1

lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>

name=No222_Z8T55K1></A><FONT size=3><SPAN class=sect2content><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><SPAN 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">如果存在下列情形，注册会计师应当视其影响的重大与广泛程度，出具保留结论或否定结论的报告：</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box" name=No223_Z8T55K1X1></A><SPAN 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>（一）注册会计师的结论提及责任方的认定，且该认定未在所有重大方面作出公允表达；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

size=3>（二）注册会计师的结论直接提及鉴证对象和标准，且鉴证对象信息存在重大错报。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN

lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><FONT 
size=3>在某些情况下，注册会计师应当考虑解除业务约定。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 7.35pt 0pt 0cm; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><SPAN

name=No230_Z8T57K1></A><FONT size=3><SPAN class=sect2content><SPAN

style="BOX-SIZING: border-box">当注册会计师针对鉴证对象信息出具报告，或同意将其姓名与鉴证对象联系在一起时，则注册会计师与该鉴证对象发生了关联。</SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN 
lang=EN-US 
style='FONT-FAMILY: "微软雅黑",sans-serif; COLOR: #333333'><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=sect2content1 
style="BOX-SIZING: border-box; BACKGROUND: white; WORD-SPACING: 0px; ORPHANS: 2; WIDOWS: 2; MARGIN: 11.25pt 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial"><A

size=3>如果获知他人不恰当地将其姓名与鉴证对象相关联，注册会计师应当要求其停止这种行为，并考虑采取其他必要的措施，包括将不恰当使用注册会计师姓名这一情况告知所有已知的使用者或征询法律意见。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=title
