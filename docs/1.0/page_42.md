<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>财会<SPAN lang=EN-US>[2006]3</SPAN>号<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt" 
align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>第一章 总则<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第一条 为了规范现金流量表的编制和列报，根据《企业会计准则——基本准则》，制定本准则。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第二条 现金流量表，是指反映企业在一定会计期间现金和现金等价物流入和流出的报表。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　现金，是指企业库存现金以及可以随时用于支付的存款。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　现金等价物，是指企业持有的期限短、流动性强、易于转换为已知金额现金、价值变动风险很小的投资。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　本准则提及现金时，除非同时提及现金等价物，均包括现金和现金等价物。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第三条 合并现金流量表的编制和列报，适用《企业会计准则第<SPAN 
lang=EN-US>33</SPAN>号——合并财务报表》。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt" 
align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>第二章 基本要求<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第四条 现金流量表应当分别经营活动、投资活动和筹资活动列报现金流量。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第五条 现金流量应当分别按照现金流入和现金流出总额列报。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　但是，下列各项可以按照净额列报：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（一）代客户收取或支付的现金。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（二）周转快、金额大、期限短项目的现金流入和现金流出。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT 
face=宋体>　　（三）金融企业的有关项目，包括短期贷款发放与收回的贷款本金、活期存款的吸收与支付、同业存款和存放同业款项的存取、向其他金融企业拆借资金、以及证券的买入与卖出等。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第六条 
自然灾害损失、保险索赔等特殊项目，应当根据其性质，分别归并到经营活动、投资活动和筹资活动现金流量类别中单独列报。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第七条 
外币现金流量以及境外子公司的现金流量，应当采用现金流量发生日的即期汇率或按照系统合理的方法确定的、与现金流量发生日即期汇率近似的汇率折算。汇率变动对现金的影响额应当作为调节项目，在现金流量表中单独列报。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt" 
align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>第三章 经营活动现金流量<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第八条 企业应当采用直接法列示经营活动产生的现金流量。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　经营活动，是指企业投资活动和筹资活动以外的所有交易和事项。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　直接法，是指通过现金收入和现金支出的主要类别列示经营活动的现金流量。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第九条 有关经营活动现金流量的信息，可以通过下列途径之一取得：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（一）企业的会计记录。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（二）根据下列项目对利润表中的营业收入、营业成本以及其他项目进行调整：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　<SPAN lang=EN-US>1. </SPAN>当期存货及经营性应收和应付项目的变动；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　<SPAN lang=EN-US>2. 
</SPAN>固定资产折旧、无形资产摊销、计提资产减值准备等其他非现金项目；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　<SPAN lang=EN-US>3. </SPAN>属于投资活动或筹资活动现金流量的其他非现金项目。<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　第十条 经营活动产生的现金流量至少应当单独列示反映下列信息的项目：<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（一）销售商品、提供劳务收到的现金；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（二）收到的税费返还；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（三）收到其他与经营活动有关的现金；<SPAN 
lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="LINE-HEIGHT: 180%; TEXT-INDENT: 24pt"><SPAN 
style="mso-ascii-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: 宋体; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体"><FONT 
size=3><FONT face=宋体>　　（四）购买商品、接受劳务支付的现金；<SPAN
